People Helping People
Problem and Hypothesis

Problem: A agency that connects volunteers with those in need are looking for a website to automate the process.
Hypothesis: Secure way for people to create requests and offer Help. Login, View/Offer, Request/Create

My Role

Design and Build a way for users to create requests and view their requests.

How We Developed a Solution

We came to our proposed solutions by utilizing user journeys and wire frame usability testing to get feedback from out class and professor.
I worked with 2 other developer/designers and we used a voting system for which ideas we would build and test.

How Our Solution Solves the Problem

Our self service model allows people to connect on-line to request and offer aid.

Challenges faced

During ideation, we had designed a flow that involved a landing page but we found it unnecessary and caused delay in getting the user to the main content.

What I learned

How to implement and use the bootstrap framework as well as the importance of communication and planning within a design and development.
